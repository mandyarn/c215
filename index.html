Christian Guémy

More text about C215 here...

“I paint anywhere I go. Portraits in the streets, under the name of C215. And I try to pay homage to the people who constitute the city, especially to those who live in its streets, like children and homeless people, turning all the anonymous into icons. My tools are stencils and spray, but I am not a vandal. My art is contextual. I strongly believe street art can physically change the city and the daily life of people living in it.” – Christian Guémy / C215
